## 2024-04-29 - Real-time Dashboard Accessibility Patterns
**Learning:** In real-time AI dashboards (like EdgeSight), applying `aria-live` broadly to all dynamically changing components overwhelms screen readers due to high-frequency telemetry updates (e.g., 10Hz FPS or gauge changes). However, low-frequency updates like alert logs or categorical risk level shifts are critical and must use `aria-live="polite"` to ensure they are announced. Additionally, text-labeled buttons with decorative icons need `aria-hidden="true"` on the icons to prevent redundant announcements.
**Action:** When auditing real-time interfaces, differentiate between high-frequency telemetry and low-frequency critical events. Apply `aria-live="polite"` strictly to low-frequency alert containers and `aria-hidden="true"` to decorative elements within interactive controls.
